# Welcome to Kart Companion

<div class="highlight">
Kart Companion is a powerful Chrome extension that enhances your Smash Karts experience with comprehensive statistics tracking, real-time HUD overlays, and detailed match analytics.
</div>

## What is Kart Companion?

Kart Companion integrates seamlessly with the Smash Karts game to provide players with:

- **Real-Time Statistics Tracking**: Monitor kills, deaths, KDR, streaks, and more during gameplay
- **Customizable HUD Overlays**: Display live stats on-screen with full customization options
- **Match History & Analytics**: View detailed match breakdowns, trends, and performance insights
- **Player Cards & Sharing**: Create and share customizable player cards with stats
- **Cross-Device Sync**: Export/import stats for backup and transfer

## Quick Start

1. **Install the Extension**: Download from the Chrome Web Store
2. **Enable Tracking**: Click the extension icon and toggle features on
3. **Play Smash Karts**: Stats are automatically tracked during matches
4. **View Your Stats**: Access detailed analytics in the extension popup
